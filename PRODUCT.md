# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Jordan (founder of Mogul Design Agency) and, later, non-technical brand owners
he onboards. They review AI-drafted marketing emails and blog posts on phone or
desktop, usually in short sessions between client work. The job on any screen:
get a draft to "good enough to send" fast, with total confidence nothing
publishes without their explicit approval.

## Product Purpose

An automated content engine: it picks a topic from a stored brand strategy,
drafts an on-brand email or blog post with Claude, routes it through a human
approval gate, and publishes to MailerLite (email) and Sanity (blog). Success
is a reviewer approving a publish-ready draft in minutes, not editing HTML.

## Brand Personality

Bold, direct, technical-but-warm. "Bold spectrum, hard contrast, zero noise":
ink-black UI, one signature spectrum gradient per view, Hot Magenta focus.
Plain-language UX copy, no marketing fluff, never em-dashes. Speaks to the
reviewer like a sharp collaborator, not a wizard.

## Anti-references

- Enterprise CMS density (endless toolbars, nested panels a la WordPress admin).
- SaaS-cream minimalism; this product is committed dark, not tinted-neutral safe.
- Chat-first AI UIs where every action is a prompt; controls should be tappable
  first, typed second.
- Anything that hides the approval gate or makes publishing feel automatic.

## Design Principles

- **Review is the product.** The draft preview is the hero of every screen;
  tools orbit it, never crowd it.
- **One decision per moment.** Progressive disclosure over dashboards of
  options; sheets and tabs, not stacked panels.
- **Honest states.** Real generation progress, real costs, real errors;
  never fake spinners or silent failures.
- **Tap before type.** Every common edit reachable as a chip/button; free-text
  is the escape hatch, not the default.
- **Mobile-first native feel.** Safe-area aware, thumb-reachable actions,
  responsively expands to sidebar layout on desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on the app UI (dark theme) and enforced on generated emails
(code-level contrast checks). Meaningful alt text is required on every content
image, generated or uploaded. Touch targets sized for mobile; reduced-motion
users get non-animated equivalents.
